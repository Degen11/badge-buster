Concept

Badge Buster

One tap to wipe every red dot off your home screen. A clean slate for your phone, and your head.

The idea

You know that creeping anxiety when your home screen is littered with red badges? 12 unread emails, 99+ messages, 5 updates you'll never install. Badge Buster's premise is dead simple — one button, all of it gone.

Why it's a concept

iOS and Android sandbox every app for security reasons, meaning no app can reach into another's notification badges and clear them. So Badge Buster will never ship — it's a design exploration of what the UX could feel like if that restriction didn't exist.

The frustration it reflects

Those little red circles are engineered to pull you back in. Most of the time they're noise, not signal. Badge Buster is a nod to the shared desire for a calmer, less demanding phone.

Built by Degen Hill  ·  A UX concept, not a shipping product.
